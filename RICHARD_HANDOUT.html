What Is SynapShield?

The Short Version (The One That Matters)

Most Parkinson's research tries to fix the brain after it's already damaged. We took a different approach.

We went to the beginning — to the gut, where Parkinson's actually starts (decades before it reaches the brain). And we built a shield.

✅ A tiny, safe gel is placed in the gut (15-minute outpatient procedure)

✅ Result: Parkinson's never starts. Never arrives. Never steals your future.

The Science (In Case You're Curious)

You don't need a PhD to understand this — but the math is beautiful, so I'll share it anyway:

Parkinson's starts when a protein called alpha-synuclein goes rogue in your gut. It then travels up a nerve (the vagus nerve) to your brain, where it kills the cells that keep you moving.

SynapShield interrupts that journey. It's a "pathological sink" — a trap that catches the toxin before it can ever reach your brain.
