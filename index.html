A Gift to Our Community

In Ocean Shores, we look out for each other. When our neighbor Richard — who runs the medical supply store and cares for his wife while managing his own Parkinson's — showed us what this disease does to a good man, we knew we had to act.

So we did something unprecedented: we took the world's most advanced Parkinson's research, and we brought it home. Not to a university lab, but to our community, where it belongs.

What if we could stop Parkinson's before it even starts?

Not 15 years too late, when the tremors have already begun. But 15 years before — when it's just a whisper in the gut, long before it reaches the brain. That's what SynapShield does.

This isn't just science. It's a promise to Richard, to his wife, to every family in Ocean Shores and beyond who's watched Parkinson's steal someone they love. We're not waiting for a cure. We're building an interception.

This technology is free. Open-source. Available to the world. Because everyone deserves a fighting chance against Parkinson's — not just those who can afford it.
