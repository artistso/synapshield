Data Availability:

All data, code, and materials supporting the findings of this study are openly available at: https://github.com/artistso/synapshield

Repository: https://github.com/artistso/synapshield
License: MIT License
DOI (software): 10.5281/zenodo.TBD
Live demo: https://artistso.github.io/synapshield/

Code Availability:

- 4-species PDE solver (Python): simulations/python/synapshield_pde_solver.py
- MATLAB solver: simulations/matlab/synapshield_pde_solver.m
- FEniCSx poroelastic: simulations/python/fenicsx_poroelastic.py
- Multiphysics integration: simulations/python/multiphysics_integration.py
- Requirements: numpy==1.26.4, scipy==1.13.1, matplotlib==3.9.0
- Runtime: ~18 seconds — 1-year validation — alpha-synuclein at nerve: 3.33e-17 mol/m^3
- Validation: >94% reduction at 7 days, >99.99% at 1 year

Datasets referenced (background context only — no individual-level data analyzed in this computational model):

- PPMI — Parkinson's Progression Markers Initiative — https://www.ppmi-info.org/
- UK Biobank — https://www.ukbiobank.ac.uk/

No individual-level human subject data were accessed or analyzed. All model parameters derived from published literature:

- Braak, H., et al. (2003). Neurobiology of Aging, 24(2), 197-211. doi:10.1016/S0197-4580(02)00065-9
- Kim, S., et al. (2019). Nature Neuroscience, 22(8), 1235-1243. doi:10.1038/s41593-019-0449-7
